Our Facilities

The Excellence University has a variety of facilities to help you study, stay fit, and participate in campus life.

World Class Library

This is one of the best-resourced academic libraries in the world, with over twenty-four million printed books and manuscripts, 70,000 distinct electronic journals, 900,000 electronic books, and several hundred databases.

Largest Play Ground

With nearly 850 student-athletes, Excellence University organizes 22 varsity sports. Two locker rooms, a cardio room, free weight room, athletic trainer room, and offices are located on the upper floor of the Excellence University Fitness Center, while the basketball/volleyball court and two locker rooms are located on the bottom level.

Tasty and Healthy Food

For our students and visitors on campus, we provide facilities such as a bar and canteen. The canteen serves a variety of dishes that are freshly cooked and produced with quality in mind. We provide our own fresh, seasonal food directly from the vegetable farm to the table, reducing transportation-related CO2 emissions. A three-course meal is available to students at a discounted rate.
