Our Courses

Courses We Offer

We offer 30 undergraduate courses in over 65 subject areas, so you'll be able to discover something that interests you.

Undergraduate

Do you enjoy asking questions and learning new things? Do you always get the best grades in your class? Are you looking for a world-class education in a setting that respects people for who they are? Excellence University could be a good fit for you.

Post Graduate

Our graduate students have the opportunity to work with world-renowned academics as well as in world-class organisations. This website is for people interested in applying for postgraduate study in 2021-22.

Continuing Education

Every year, almost 40,000 people enroll in courses offered by Excellence University's Department of Continuing Education, making it one of the world's largest providers of continuing education.

Our Facilities

The Excellence University has a variety of facilities to help you study, stay fit, and participate in campus life.

World Class Library

This is one of the best-resourced academic libraries in the world, with over twenty-four million printed books and manuscripts, 70,000 distinct electronic journals, 900,000 electronic books, and several hundred databases.

Largest Play Ground

With nearly 850 student-athletes, Excellence University organizes 22 varsity sports. Two locker rooms, a cardio room, free weight room, athletic trainer room, and offices are located on the upper floor of the Excellence University Fitness Center, while the basketball/volleyball court and two locker rooms are located on the bottom level.

Tasty and Healthy Food

For our students and visitors on campus, we provide facilities such as a bar and canteen. The canteen serves a variety of dishes that are freshly cooked and produced with quality in mind. We provide our own fresh, seasonal food directly from the vegetable farm to the table, reducing transportation-related CO2 emissions. A three-course meal is available to students at a discounted rate.

About Us

Excellence University is a Top 20 public university with a global reputation for fostering true talent and innovation.
Excellence University combines a high level of research activity with a high level of social mobility
to elevate and expedite learner success in a global community by focusing
on the environment, health, technology, and justice.
Excellence University has a student body of around 689,000 students and 466,000 alumni.
